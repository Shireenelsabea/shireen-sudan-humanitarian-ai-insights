Can AI help humanitarian teams turn crisis signals into action?

I created **Sudan Humanitarian AI Insights**, a responsible AI dashboard prototype that explores how humanitarian teams could use data analysis and AI-assisted triage to support needs prioritization, community feedback review, and decision-support briefs.

What the prototype demonstrates:

- Hotspot ranking for humanitarian prioritization
- Synthetic feedback classification
- Sector pressure analysis
- Trend and scenario views
- AI Action Briefs for selected locations

No paid APIs. No private field data. No personally identifiable information.

The goal is not to replace humanitarian judgment. The goal is to show how AI can support teams with clearer signals, transparent scoring, and human validation.

I’m open to collaboration with NGOs, humanitarian data teams, and social-impact organizations working on responsible AI, community feedback, and needs analysis.
