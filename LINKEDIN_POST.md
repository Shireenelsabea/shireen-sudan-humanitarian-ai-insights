Use case: turning displacement context, needs indicators, and community feedback into a clearer action brief for NGO decision-makers.

What it shows:

- Needs pressure across food, health, shelter, WASH, and protection
- Local NLP-style classification of community feedback
- Trend and scenario views for planning conversations
- An AI Action Brief that turns analysis into practical next steps

No paid APIs.
No private data.
No personally identifiable information.

This is not meant to replace field teams.

It is a decision-support layer:
to help teams listen faster, prioritize more transparently, and explain why a location may need attention now.

The same approach can be adapted to any region or dataset:
assessment forms, community feedback, distribution logs, call center notes, public humanitarian datasets, or NGO monitoring data.
